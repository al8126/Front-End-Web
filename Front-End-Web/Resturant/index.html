Udon St. Marks

Cozy, cash only, and otherworldy

Nestled in one of East Village's most notable street, street lights blistered across retail stores and resturants lays Udon St. Marks. Here, you can grab a homey meal of either steaming udon or rice inside the hazy, dim glow of the lights that paint across the wood grain bar. Remember to bring cash, as only the best homey resturants will be cash only.
